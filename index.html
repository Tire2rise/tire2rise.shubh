About Tier2Rise

Tire2rise is a purpose-driven platform committed to unlocking the potential of students from Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities across India. We believe that talent knows no boundaries, and every student deserves access to the same opportunities—regardless of their location or background.

Our mission is to identify, nurture, and connect skilled individuals with top-tier companies through a digital-first ecosystem that offers:

  • Skill development
  • Mentorship
  • Career guidance
  • Job placement

How It Works

  1. Discover: Online assessments and challenges to identify top talent.
  2. Learn: Curated courses and live mentorship from industry professionals.
  3. Connect: Resume building, mock interviews, and career counseling.
  4. Launch: Direct hiring partnerships with companies.

Our Programs

We offer career support and training programs for students from Tier-2, Tier-3, and Tier-4 cities.

Our Vision

To become the bridge between hidden talent and hiring companies, reducing hiring time and increasing access to opportunity — one student at a time.
